About Us

Welcome to Odina Offset Printing Press, the first Offset Printing Press in Kashmir.

Since 1947, Odina Offset Printing Press is one of the leading Printing Presses as quality printers of Jammu & Kashmir.

Odina Offset Printing Press is a leading provider of Printing Services to the smallest to largest incorporates, institutions, hospitals, exporters etc., spread all across India. Our Convenient, Cost-effective, Timesaving and High Quality Offset Printing Services have certainly helped us not only in winning Good Clientele but also to share a long term relationship with them.
